Ectopy Monitor – Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 30, 2026

Overview

Ectopy Monitor (“the app”) is designed to run entirely on your device. It does not collect, transmit, or sell your personal data. All processing is performed locally on your device.

Data we collect

The app does not collect any personally identifiable information (PII) such as your name, email address, phone number, or location.

Heart rate and related data

The app may read heart rate and related timing data from compatible Bluetooth devices that you explicitly connect. This data is used only to display and analyze heart rhythm information on your device.

Local storage

The app may store session data and analysis results in the app’s private storage area on your device. These files are not accessible to other apps under normal operating conditions and are not automatically shared with anyone.

Data sharing and transmission

The app does not:

  • Send your data to external servers.
  • Use cloud backup or remote storage for app data.
  • Share data with third parties for advertising, analytics, or marketing.

Advertising and analytics

The app does not include third‑party advertising and does not use third‑party analytics SDKs.

Bluetooth and permissions

The app uses Bluetooth‑related permissions solely to connect to compatible heart rate devices that you choose. These permissions are used only for device communication and not for tracking your location or identity.

Security

Because all processing and storage occur locally on your device, the security of your data also depends on the security of your device and operating system. We recommend using a device lock (PIN, password, or biometric) and keeping your operating system up to date.

Children

The app is not directed to children under 13 and is intended for use by adults or under the supervision of a healthcare professional or guardian, where applicable.

Changes to this policy

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. When changes are made, the “Last updated” date at the top of this page will be revised. Continued use of the app after changes are published constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy or how the app handles data, you can contact the developer using the contact details provided in the app’s listing on Google Play.
