Beat Timing Viewer - Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 24, 2026

Overview

Beat Timing Viewer is designed to run entirely on the device. The app does not collect, transmit, or sell personal data to the developer. Processing of beat-to-beat timing and related session data occurs locally on the device.

Data the app does not collect

The app does not collect personally identifiable information (PII) such as name, email address, phone number, or location.

Data processed on the device

Heart rate and RR-interval timing

The app may read heart rate and RR-interval timing data from compatible Bluetooth devices that are explicitly paired through the app. This data is used only to display charts, compute timing classifications, and build session summaries on the device.

Local storage

Session data, timing classifications, user-entered notes, and analysis results may be stored in the app's private storage area on the device. Beat and summary files are stored in encrypted form under normal operating conditions. These files are not accessible to other apps under normal operating conditions and are not automatically transmitted elsewhere.

Data sharing and transmission

The app does not:

  • Send session or timing data to developer-operated servers.
  • Use cloud backup or remote storage for app data.
  • Share data with third parties for advertising, analytics, or marketing.

User-initiated export

The app may create decrypted plain-text files when export or share is initiated from the app. Exported beat files include automatic Classification tags and a separate User note column for manually entered text. Once exported or shared outside the app, timing and heart-rate data may be visible to others or third-party services depending on the destination chosen. That sharing remains at the exporter's discretion and risk.

Advertising and analytics

The app does not include third-party advertising and does not use third-party analytics SDKs.

Bluetooth and permissions

Bluetooth-related permissions are used solely to connect to compatible heart-rate monitors selected in the app. These permissions are used for device communication only and not for location or identity tracking.

Security

Because processing and storage occur locally on the device, data security also depends on device and operating-system protections. A device lock (PIN, password, or biometric) and current operating-system updates are recommended.

Children

The app is not directed to children under 13 and is intended for adults or for use under appropriate supervision where applicable.

Changes to this policy

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. When changes are made, the Last updated date at the top of this page will be revised. Continued use of the app after changes are published constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

Questions about this privacy policy or how the app handles data may be directed to the developer using the contact details provided in the app's store listing (Google Play or the App Store).
